反转序列的可读性和性能

Fri 04 September 2009
By anrs
Category: blog

老规矩，直接上图上真相…

代码：

#!/usr/bin/env python
#coding=utf-8

rev_by_index = lambda x: x[::-1]
rev_by_func = lambda x: [e for e in reversed(x)]

if __name__ == ‘__main__’:
    for i in xrange(10, 20001):
        lst = range(i)
        rev_by_index(lst)
        rev_by_func(lst)
